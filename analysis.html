On the rest of this page there are lists of text-filled, geometric pdf documents. Each is a long, computer-generated sequence of images and texts that arise from a core algorithmic seed. They are in black, white, red & yellow. The drawings consist of circles and fragmented lines. The texts are generated from the source material. The original text was sliced, word occurances counted and new texts were spun from that information : like a machine encountering the text / reading the text. See ::: a screen reader optimized text page that maps the symbol-laced original text into something easier for a screen reader to decipher. I am trying to learn more about accessibility. Thank you for your patience as I hone my skills.
